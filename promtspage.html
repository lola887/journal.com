self-reflection prompts

  • What are three things you love about yourself?
  • How do you handle stress, and how can you improve?
  • Describe a time when you felt truly at peace. What were you doing?
  • What’s a lesson you’ve learned recently that you want to remember forever?
  • What are your biggest fears, and how do they hold you back?

emotional health prompts

  • How are you feeling today? Be honest.
  • What’s something that has been weighing on your mind lately?
  • Write a letter to your future self. What do you hope they’ve achieved or learned?
  • What’s one way you can show yourself kindness today?
  • What’s a recent event that made you feel overwhelmed? How did you cope?
